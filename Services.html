Simplify Compliance, Focus on Growth!

Federal Taxation(IRS)

Even as a disregarded entity, your LLC must still file certain federal forms if it has a U.S. presence or engages in some types of U.S. transactions. Specifically, you’d need to file Form 5472 with a pro forma Form 1120 if you have reportable transactions with foreign owners or related parties.

State Taxes

Depending on the state where your LLC is established, you may have state-level tax filing requirements. It’s crucial to check the specific requirements for the state in which your LLC is registered.

ITIN or SSN

If you’re required to file U.S. taxes, you would generally need an Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN).

Annual Reporting

You are required to file an informational return to the IRS yearly, regardless of your LLC’s activity levels, to maintain your conformity and avoid potential penalties.
